**SYNTHETIC EPC DEMO DATA** | **Project ID:** `atlas-demo-dc-01` | **Status:** Answered

## Page 1

**Question:** The UPS-A bypass cabinet is adjacent to the north wall. Please confirm the minimum front maintenance clearance required for removal and bypass operation.  
**Answer:** Maintain 900 mm minimum clear working space at the UPS-A front service face, including the bypass cabinet operating position.  
**Reference:** `UPS_Specification.md`, p. 2, clause 2.2.5.

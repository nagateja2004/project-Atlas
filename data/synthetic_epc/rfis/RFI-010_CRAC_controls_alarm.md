## Page 1

**Question:** Should CRAC common alarm include both condensate and fan failure?  
**Answer:** Yes. Expose condensate alarm, fan status, supply-air temperature, and common alarm to the project controls gateway.  
**Reference:** `CRAC_Specification.md`, p. 2, clauses 2.3.3 and 2.4.1.

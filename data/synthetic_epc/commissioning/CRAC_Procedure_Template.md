# Synthetic Commissioning Procedure Template — CRAC-1

**SYNTHETIC EPC DEMO DATA — NOT AN OFFICIAL TEST STANDARD**  
**Project ID:** `atlas-demo-dc-01` | **Document ID:** `PROC-CRAC-001`

## Page 1

### Preconditions

1. Confirm chilled-water piping is flushed, treated according to the project’s synthetic water plan, and leak tested.
2. Verify a minimum 24-inch front coil/filter service clearance.
3. Verify supply power, condensate routing, controls network, and common alarm wiring.

### Functional sequence

1. Start the unit and trend return-air temperature, supply-air temperature, fan speed, valve position, and condensate alarm.
2. Simulate fan-status and condensate alarms and confirm the common alarm reaches the synthetic controls gateway.
3. Compare demonstrated sensible capacity against the selected vendor’s submitted performance record.

## Page 2

### Acceptance record

Record design condition, water temperatures, airflow indication, alarm results, exceptions, and corrective action owner. This template is only a demo procedure and makes no claim about third-party commissioning criteria.

# Synthetic Commissioning Procedure Template — UPS-A

**SYNTHETIC EPC DEMO DATA — NOT AN OFFICIAL TEST STANDARD**  
**Project ID:** `atlas-demo-dc-01` | **Document ID:** `PROC-UPS-001`

## Page 1

### Preconditions

1. Approved synthetic submittal, one-line diagram, and battery calculation are available.
2. Installation inspection, torque records, grounding continuity, and controls network addressing are complete.
3. Maintain the project-specified 900 mm front working space throughout the test.

### Functional sequence

1. Verify nameplate voltage, module quantity, battery string quantity, and firmware version.
2. Start the UPS in normal double-conversion mode; record input/output voltage, frequency, load, and alarms.
3. Transfer to static bypass and return to normal mode. Record transfer result and active alarms.

## Page 2

### Battery autonomy demonstration

1. Apply a controlled load representative of 100 percent rated load for the agreed demonstration interval.
2. Record initial battery voltage, elapsed time, alarms, and final voltage.
3. Confirm the submitted battery calculation supports the synthetic 15-minute design autonomy; this procedure does not certify an external standard.

### Acceptance record

Record pass/fail, exceptions, corrective action owner, and cited source documents by page.

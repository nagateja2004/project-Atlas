### Preconditions

1. Confirm approved synthetic submittal, factory test record, shipping-section inspection, and torque records are available.
2. Verify source, tie, and grounding key interlocks against the synthetic one-line diagram.
3. Confirm the equipment room operating aisle is clear before testing.

### Electrical checks

1. Perform insulation-resistance test, phasing verification, and protective-device functional trip checks.
2. Verify nameplate voltage, bus rating, and interrupting rating against the approved synthetic submittal.
3. Confirm metering communications through the vendor gateway using Modbus TCP.

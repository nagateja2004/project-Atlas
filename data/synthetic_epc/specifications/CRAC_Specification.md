### 2.3 Capacity and electrical requirements

**2.3.1** Minimum net sensible cooling capacity: 120 kW at 24 °C return-air dry bulb and the scheduled chilled-water condition.

**2.3.2** Electrical supply: 480 V, three-phase, 60 Hz. Provide dual independent control-power inputs where offered by the selected manufacturer.

**2.3.3** Fans: electronically commutated, variable speed, with fan-status alarm exposed to the project controls gateway.

**2.3.4** Provide a factory performance report using the manufacturer’s declared test method. This is a synthetic project documentation requirement, not a claim of a third-party certification.

### 2.4 Installation requirements

**2.4.1** Provide condensate alarm, chilled-water leak detection input, supply-air temperature sensor, and common alarm contact.

**2.4.2** Maintain not less than 24 inches of clear service access at the front coil/filter access face.

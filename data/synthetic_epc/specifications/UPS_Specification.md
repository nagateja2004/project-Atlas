### 26 33 53 — Uninterruptible Power Supply System

1.1 Provide one modular, online double-conversion UPS for the Atlas Demo Data Hall. The selected system shall serve the critical IT distribution board UPS-A. This project specification is a synthetic demonstration requirement and is not a standard or code.

1.2 Submittals shall identify the offered manufacturer, model, module arrangement, batteries, bypass equipment, heat rejection, and proposed service clearances. State each deviation explicitly; silence shall be treated as confirmation of compliance for demo evaluation.

1.3 Factory witness testing shall include a full-load functional run, static-bypass transfer, battery discharge evidence, and alarm verification before shipment.

## Page 2

### 2.2 Electrical and performance requirements

**2.2.1** Nominal system rating: 100 kVA / 90 kW minimum, 480/277 V, three-phase, four-wire, 60 Hz input and output.

**2.2.2** Architecture: online double-conversion with a minimum N+1 power-module arrangement. Failure or isolation of one power module shall not reduce available capacity below 90 kW.

**2.2.3** Harmonic performance: input current THDi not more than 5 percent at full load; output voltage THDv not more than 3 percent for linear load.

**2.2.4** Battery autonomy: not less than 15 minutes at 100 percent rated load at the design ambient condition of 25 °C.

**2.2.5** Provide a maintenance bypass and make-before-break transfer arrangement. Provide a minimum 900 mm clear working space at the front service face.

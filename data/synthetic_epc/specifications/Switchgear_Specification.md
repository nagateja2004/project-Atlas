### 2.2 Electrical requirements

**2.2.1** System voltage: 480Y/277 V, three-phase, four-wire, 60 Hz.

**2.2.2** Main bus: 4,000 A continuous, copper, with a main-tie-main arrangement and drawout breakers.

**2.2.3** Short-circuit interrupting rating: not less than 65 kAIC at 480 V for all main and feeder protective devices.

**2.2.4** Form of separation: Form 3b equivalent compartment segregation for this synthetic project.

### 2.3 Safety and enclosure requirements

**2.3.1** Provide a front-accessible, indoor lineup with a minimum 1,200 mm operating aisle.

**2.3.2** Provide key interlocks for source, tie, and grounding sequence as indicated on the synthetic one-line diagram.

**2.3.3** Provide a manufacturer test report for each shipping section.

**2.3.4** The project-specific enclosure requirement is arc-resistant Type 2B. This is a fictional requirement for comparison testing only; it is not an assertion about external certification criteria.
